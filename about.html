About Us

Safety first.
Always.

RequestHer was built by women who were tired of feeling unsafe in their own homes. We created the platform we always wished existed.

Our Story

Why we built this

Every woman has a story — a time she felt uncomfortable letting a stranger into her home, or hesitated to book a service because she couldn't be sure who would show up. We've all been there. RequestHer started from that shared experience.

We built a platform where every provider is verified, vetted, and background-checked. Where your phone number stays private. Where a trusted contact can follow your appointment in real time. Not as features — as foundations.

RequestHer is launching in Chicago with a curated network of women service providers across cleaning, home repair, beauty, wellness, tech, and more. Our goal is simple: make it easy and safe for women to access the services they need.

For our providers, we offer a platform that respects their time, protects their privacy, and connects them with clients who specifically seek out women-owned businesses. We grow together.
